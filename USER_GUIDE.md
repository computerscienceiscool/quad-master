## Getting Started

### What is Quad Master?

Quad Master is a unique productivity app that helps you organize your life into four meaningful areas (quadrants). Unlike traditional to-do lists, Quad Master helps you maintain balance across all areas of your life while building lasting habits through streak tracking and smart reminders.

When you first open Quad Master, you'll create your personalized board:

1. **Name Your Quadrants** (4 steps)

   - These represent the four main areas of your life

2. **Common Quadrant Examples**
   - **Life Balance**: Body, Mind, Career, Relationships
   - **Student**: Academics, Career Prep, Health, Social
   - **Entrepreneur**: Product, Sales, Team, Health
   - **Parent**: Kids, Self-Care, House, Partner
   - **Creative**: Create, Learn, Share, Rest

## The Basics

### Adding Tasks

1. **Tap a Quadrant** to open its task list
2. **Tap "add new"** at the bottom
3. **Type your task** and press Enter
4. Your task appears in the list

**Example**: In "Health" quadrant, add "Morning run"

### Daily vs Weekly Tasks

Each quadrant can have two types of tasks:

**Daily Tasks** üåÖ
- Reset every day at midnight
- Perfect for: habits, routines, daily goals
- Examples: "Exercise", "Read 10 pages", "Call a friend"

**Weekly Tasks** üìÖ
- Reset every Sunday at midnight
- Perfect for: bigger goals, less frequent activities
- Examples: "Meal prep", "Review finances", "Date night"

**Switching Views**: Tap the footer bar to switch between Daily and Weekly

**Method 1: Quick Complete**

**Method 2: From Task Menu**
- Long-press any task

1. Immediately after completing, an undo toast appears at the bottom
2. Tap "UNDO" within 4 seconds
3. Task reappears uncompleted

üí° **Tip**: Navigate away from the screen to dismiss the undo toast

1. **Long-press** the task

## Streaks & History

### What Are Streaks?

A streak is how many days (or weeks) in a row you've completed a task. Streaks help you build lasting habits by visualizing your consistency.

### How Streaks Work

**Daily Task Streaks**
- Complete the task today ‚Üí Streak increases
- Skip a day ‚Üí Streak resets to 0
- Complete yesterday AND today ‚Üí Streak continues

**Weekly Task Streaks**
- Complete the task this week ‚Üí Streak increases
- Skip a week ‚Üí Streak resets to 0
- Complete last week AND this week ‚Üí Streak continues

### Streak Levels

Streaks are color-coded by achievement level:

üî• **Blue** (1-6 days/weeks)
- You're getting started
- Keep the momentum going!

üí™ **Green** (7-13 days/weeks)
- One week strong!
- You're building a habit

‚≠ê **Orange** (14-29 days/weeks)
- Two weeks or more!
- You're crushing it

üèÜ **Purple** (30+ days/weeks)
- A full month or more!
- You're a champion

### Viewing Streaks

**On Task Lists:**
- Look for the colored badge next to task names
- Shows emoji + number (e.g., üî•7)

**Task Details:**
1. Long-press any task
2. See streak info in the bottom sheet
3. View total completions

**Weekly Summary:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Weekly Summary
2. See average streaks per quadrant
3. See your best streak for each quadrant

### Completion History

Every time you complete a task, it's recorded:
- Date and time saved
- Last 90 days kept (older entries auto-deleted)
- Used for streak calculation
- Visible in long-press menu

### Protecting Your Streaks

**Delete Warning:**

## Swipe Actions & Gestures

### What Are Swipe Actions?

Swipe actions let you quickly perform common tasks by swiping left or right on any task item, without opening menus.

### How to Use Swipe Actions

**Swipe Right (Green Background)**
- Action: Complete Task
- Use: Quick completion without tapping checkbox
- Speed: Fastest way to mark tasks done

**Swipe Left (Blue/Red Background)**
- First action (Blue): Save as Template
- Second action (Red): Delete Task
- Use: Quick access to secondary actions

### Step-by-Step

**To Complete a Task:**
1. Find the task in any list
2. Swipe right (from left edge toward right)
3. Task disappears and undo toast appears
4. You'll feel a vibration on supported devices

**To Save as Template:**
1. Find the task you want to save
2. Swipe left (from right edge toward left)
3. Tap the blue "Template" button
4. Confirmation message appears

**To Delete a Task:**
1. Find the task to delete
2. Swipe left fully
3. Tap the red "Delete" button
4. Confirm deletion in dialog

### Tips for Swipe Actions

**Learning Curve**
- First time: Swipe slowly to see the actions
- After practice: Quick swipes work perfectly
- If you swipe too far by accident, release and it cancels

**When to Use**
- Completing tasks: Always use swipe right (fastest)
- Saving templates: Swipe left when building your library
- Deleting: Swipe left for quick access (but still has confirmation)

**Alternative Methods**
Don't like swiping? You can still:
- Tap checkbox to complete
- Long-press task for full menu
- Use the edit/delete icons in menu

### Haptic Feedback

When you perform actions, you'll feel vibrations (if your device supports it):

**Medium Vibration**
- Completing a task
- Provides satisfying confirmation

**Heavy Vibration**
- Reaching streak milestones (7, 14, 30, 50, 100 days)
- Special celebration feedback

**Light Vibration**
- Undoing a completion
- Deleting a task
- Subtle warning or confirmation

**No Vibration?**
- Check your device settings
- Older devices may not support haptics
- Battery saver mode may disable vibrations

## Troubleshooting

### Tasks Not Saving

**Problem**: Added task but it disappeared

**Solutions:**
1. Check if you pressed Enter or the checkmark
2. Verify you're not in the wrong frequency (Daily vs Weekly)
3. Task may be in wrong quadrant - check all four
4. Restart the app if issue persists

### Streaks Not Calculating

**Problem**: Completed task multiple days but streak shows 0

**Solutions:**
1. Verify you're completing (tapping checkbox), not just viewing
2. Check that you didn't skip a day
3. Long-press task to see completion history
4. Streaks reset if you skip a period

### Notifications Not Appearing

**Problem**: Not receiving notifications

**Solutions:**
1. Check device Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Quad Master
2. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
3. Check if Do Not Disturb is active
4. Verify notification toggles are ON in app settings
5. Try tapping "Test Notification" in Settings

### Templates Not Showing

**Problem**: Created template but can't find it

**Solutions:**
1. Check Settings ‚Üí Task Templates to see all templates
2. Verify template is for correct frequency (Daily/Weekly)
3. Templates show in the quadrant where created
4. Restart app if issue persists

### Swipe Actions Not Working

**Problem**: Swiping doesn't reveal action buttons

**Solutions:**
1. Swipe slowly the first few times to learn the gesture
2. Make sure you're swiping horizontally (not vertically)
3. Try swiping from the edge of the screen
4. Some lists may not support swipe (check if you're on correct screen)
5. Restart app if gesture stops responding

### No Haptic Feedback

**Problem**: Not feeling vibrations when completing tasks

**Solutions:**
1. Check device Settings ‚Üí Sounds & Haptics
2. Ensure "System Haptics" is enabled
3. Older devices may not support haptic feedback
4. Battery saver mode may disable haptics
5. Not all Android devices support haptics

### Widget Not Updating

**Problem**: Widget shows old or incorrect data

**Solutions:**
1. Open the app (forces widget update)
2. Remove and re-add widget
3. Check if app has background refresh enabled
4. iOS: Settings ‚Üí Quad Master ‚Üí Background App Refresh
5. Android: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Quad Master ‚Üí Background data
6. Widget updates every 15-30 minutes (OS limitation)

### Widget Not Showing

**Problem**: Can't find Quad Master widget

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure app is fully installed (not in "offload" mode)
2. Restart device
3. Update to latest app version
4. iOS: Check if widgets are enabled in Screen Time
5. Android: Check if widget is compatible with launcher

### Quick Actions Not Appearing

**Problem**: Long-press on app icon doesn't show menu

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you're pressing firmly (not just tapping)
2. iOS: 3D Touch or Haptic Touch must be enabled in settings
3. Android: Requires Android 7.1 or newer
4. Try pressing and holding for 1-2 seconds
5. Update to latest OS version

### Progress Ring Not Showing

**Problem**: Center circle doesn't show colored ring

**Solutions:**
1. Verify you have daily tasks created
2. Ring only appears when daily tasks exist
3. Ring may be very thin if you haven't created many tasks
4. Restart app
5. Check if you're on the home screen (not other screens)

### Task Count Badges Wrong

**Problem**: Badge shows incorrect number

**Solutions:**
1. Complete or delete a task to trigger update
2. Navigate away from home and back
3. Restart the app
4. Verify tasks aren't in "completed" state
5. Badge only shows uncompleted tasks

### App Running Slow

**Problem**: App feels sluggish

**Solutions:**
1. Too many old tasks? History auto-cleans at 90 days
2. Try deleting completed tasks manually
3. Restart the app
4. Update to latest version
5. Clear device cache
6. Check available storage space

### Data Not Migrating

**Problem**: Updated app but old data missing

**Solutions:**
1. Data should auto-migrate on first launch
2. Check if you're logged into correct account (if sync enabled)
3. Look for backup file in device storage
4. Contact support with device model and OS version

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Can I have more than 4 quadrants?**
A: No, the four-quadrant philosophy is core to the app. Choose your four most important life areas.

**Q: Can I change quadrant names after setup?**
A: Yes! Settings ‚Üí Quadrants ‚Üí Tap any quadrant ‚Üí Edit name

**Q: Can I change quadrant colors?**
A: Currently no, but this is a planned feature.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**
A: Currently, data is stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is a planned future feature.

### Tasks & Completion

**Q: What happens to completed tasks?**
A: They disappear from the list but are saved in your history (last 90 days).

**Q: Can I see completed tasks?**
A: Yes, completion history is visible in long-press menu (last 90 days).

**Q: Can I have subtasks?**
A: Not yet - this is a planned feature for future updates.

**Q: What if I forget to complete a task?**
A: You can't complete tasks from past days. Daily tasks reset at midnight, weekly tasks at Sunday midnight.

**Q: Can I complete tomorrow's tasks today?**
A: No - tasks are only completable during their designated period.

### Streaks

**Q: Do streaks carry over between daily and weekly?**
A: No - each task has its own independent streak based on its frequency.

**Q: What's the longest possible streak?**
A: Unlimited! Some users have 100+ day streaks.

**Q: Can I reset my streak?**
A: Delete and recreate the task. But why would you?

**Q: Do streaks continue if I uninstall the app?**
A: Only if you reinstall and restore data before the next completion period.

### Templates

**Q: How many templates can I create?**
A: Unlimited templates per quadrant.

**Q: Can I share templates with others?**
A: Not yet - this is planned for future updates (template marketplace!).

**Q: Can I edit a template after creating tasks from it?**
A: Yes, but editing a template doesn't change existing tasks created from it.

**Q: Can I convert a template between Daily and Weekly?**
A: Delete and recreate the template with desired frequency.

### Notifications

**Q: Can I have more than 3 notifications per day?**
A: Currently no - three is the sweet spot between helpful and annoying.

**Q: Will notifications show even if I completed all tasks?**
A: No - if you have zero remaining tasks, no notifications are sent.

**Q: Can I customize notification messages?**
A: Not yet, but messages adapt based on your progress.

**Q: Do notifications work offline?**
A: Yes! They're scheduled locally on your device.

### Swipe Actions

**Q: Can I disable swipe actions?**
A: No, but you can ignore them and use tap/long-press instead.

**Q: Can I customize which actions appear?**
A: No, the swipe actions are predefined for consistency.

**Q: What if I accidentally swipe?**
A: Release before tapping the action button and it cancels. Delete also has confirmation.

**Q: Do swipe actions work on all screens?**
A: Yes, they work on any screen showing task lists.

### Haptic Feedback

**Q: Can I turn off vibrations?**
A: Yes - use your device's system settings to disable haptics entirely.

**Q: Why don't I feel vibrations?**
A: Check device settings, battery saver mode, or device may not support haptics.

**Q: Are different vibrations intentional?**
A: Yes - medium for complete, heavy for milestones, light for undo/delete.

### Widgets

**Q: Can I complete tasks from the widget?**
A: Not yet - tap the widget to open the app and complete tasks there.

**Q: How often does the widget update?**

**Q: Can I customize what the widget shows?**
A: Not yet - widget shows up to 5 uncompleted daily tasks.

**Q: Does the widget drain battery?**
A: Minimal impact - less than 0.5% per day.

**Q: Can I have multiple widgets?**
A: Yes - add as many Quad Master widgets as you want to your home screen.

A: No - the four actions are predefined based on most common use cases.

**Q: Do quick actions work when app is closed?**
A: Yes - they launch the app and navigate to the selected feature.

### Progress Ring

**Q: Does the ring include weekly tasks?**
A: No - only daily tasks count toward the progress ring.

**Q: Can I hide the progress ring?**
